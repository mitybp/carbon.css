Padding and Margin

<element class="[p or m]-[optional: the side]"></element>

Adds the padding (p) or/and the margin (m) to element. See the padding/margin sides below.

  • p/m - all sides
  • px/mx - horizontal
  • py/my - vertical
  • pt/mt - top
  • pb/mb - bottom
  • ps/ms - start
  • pe/me - end
